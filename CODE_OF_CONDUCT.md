# TXLOGPAY Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

TXLOGPAY is committed to building an open, respectful and professional environment for contributors, collaborators and community members.

We aim to foster:

* respectful collaboration
* constructive technical discussions
* inclusive participation
* professional communication
* operational integrity

All participants are expected to contribute to a healthy and productive environment.

---

# Expected Behavior

Contributors and participants are expected to:

* communicate professionally and respectfully
* provide constructive technical feedback
* respect different viewpoints and experiences
* focus discussions on technical and operational improvement
* collaborate in good faith
* maintain professional standards during interactions

---

# Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

* harassment or discrimination
* offensive or abusive language
* personal attacks
* intimidation or threats
* trolling or disruptive behavior
* sharing confidential or sensitive information without authorization
* intentionally damaging project integrity

---

# Technical Discussions

TXLOGPAY encourages:

* architecture discussions
* infrastructure proposals
* operational workflow improvements
* constructive engineering debate

Technical disagreements should remain:

* respectful
* objective
* solution-oriented

---

# Professional Environment

TXLOGPAY is positioned as:

* operational finance infrastructure
* enterprise-oriented settlement infrastructure
* programmable trade finance platform

Participants are expected to preserve:

* professionalism
* operational focus
* institutional standards

across all interactions and contributions.

---

# Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

* GitHub repositories
* discussions
* pull requests
* issue tracking
* community interactions
* collaborative environments related to TXLOGPAY

---

# Enforcement

Project maintainers reserve the right to:

* remove inappropriate content
* moderate discussions
* restrict participation
* reject contributions that violate this Code of Conduct

Enforcement decisions will prioritize:

* community integrity
* professional collaboration
* operational continuity

---

# Reporting

If you experience or witness inappropriate behavior, contributors are encouraged to contact the project maintainers through official repository communication channels.

Reports should include:

* description of the incident
* relevant context
* supporting information if available

All reports will be handled professionally and confidentially whenever possible.

---

# Project Philosophy

TXLOGPAY values:

* operational clarity
* infrastructure quality
* professional engineering standards
* respectful collaboration
* long-term architectural thinking

The project was created to explore how operational workflows can evolve into programmable financial infrastructure.

We encourage contributors who share these principles.
