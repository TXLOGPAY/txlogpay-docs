# Operational Workflow

## Step 1 — Operation Creation

The importer creates a protected international operation containing:

* exporter information
* operational data
* financial amount
* operational release conditions

---

## Step 2 — Guarantee Reservation

The protected operational amount is reserved through the guarantee workflow.

Operational proof may include:

* PIX
* wire transfer
* SWIFT transfer

The protected amount remains operationally reserved until operational conditions are satisfied.

---

## Step 3 — Operational Validation

The operation progresses through operational milestones.

Examples:

* exporter confirmation
* cargo shipment
* customs arrival
* customs release

Operational progression becomes visible through the operational timeline infrastructure.

---

## Step 4 — Conditional Financial Release

Once operational conditions are satisfied:

* settlement execution is triggered
* treasury orchestration begins
* operational release workflows execute

The operational workflow becomes programmable financial logic.
