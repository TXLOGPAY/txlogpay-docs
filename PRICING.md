# TXLOGPAY Pricing Strategy

## Overview

TXLOGPAY pricing infrastructure was designed specifically for international trade finance workflows.

The platform monetizes:

* operational payment protection
* programmable settlement orchestration
* international treasury automation
* operational monitoring
* event-driven financial release

The pricing model intentionally aligns platform revenue with customer operational growth.

TXLOGPAY only scales when client trade volume scales.

---

# Core Pricing Philosophy

The TXLOGPAY pricing structure is based on the principle that:

* larger protected operations generate larger operational responsibility
* larger international flows require more treasury orchestration
* operational complexity increases with trade volume
* settlement confidence creates measurable financial value

For this reason, TXLOGPAY uses a percentage-based operational pricing model.

---

# Why Percentage Pricing

## Operational Alignment

A percentage-based model creates direct alignment between:

* customer growth
* operational volume
* platform infrastructure usage
* treasury orchestration complexity

The platform grows proportionally with the customer’s international trade activity.

---

## Why Not Flat Fees

Flat fee structures create operational distortion.

For small operations:

* flat fees become prohibitively expensive

For enterprise operations:

* flat fees fail to capture operational risk and infrastructure value

A protected operation of:

* USD 50k
  and:
* USD 5M

do not generate the same operational exposure, treasury complexity or settlement responsibility.

# Protected Amount Logic

TXLOGPAY operational fees are intentionally separated from protected operational value.

```text id="eq1"
Total Payment
=
Protected Operation Amount
+
Operational Fee
```

Example:

| Component                  | Value      |
| -------------------------- | ---------- |
| Protected Operation Amount | USD 10,000 |
| TXLOGPAY Fee               | USD 150    |
| Total Payment              | USD 10,150 |

In this model:

* USD 10,000 remains reserved for exporter settlement
* USD 150 becomes TXLOGPAY operational revenue

Protected operational value is never reduced by platform fees.
