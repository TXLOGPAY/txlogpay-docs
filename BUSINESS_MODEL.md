# TXLOGPAY Business Model

## Executive Overview

TXLOGPAY is a programmable trade finance infrastructure focused on operationally-triggered international settlement flows.

The platform was designed to reduce operational friction, improve payment confidence and automate financial release orchestration within international trade operations.

TXLOGPAY operates at the intersection of:

* trade finance
* operational treasury infrastructure
* programmable settlement
* operational event orchestration
* international payment automation

---

# Core Problem

International trade operations still depend on fragmented and highly manual financial processes.

Common operational pain points include:

* delayed international settlement
* lack of payment visibility
* low trust between importers and exporters
* operational uncertainty
* fragmented customs workflows
* manual financial reconciliation
* operational disputes
* inefficient guarantee management

Traditional solutions frequently rely on:

* manual escrow processes
* intermediary-heavy settlement
* delayed banking confirmation
* fragmented operational systems
* high operational overhead
