# TXLOGPAY Security Model

## Overview

TXLOGPAY was designed as an operational trade finance infrastructure platform with security as a foundational architectural principle.

The platform handles:

* operational payment workflows
* protected settlement flows
* treasury orchestration
* operational guarantees

* international trade operations

For this reason, the infrastructure was designed around:
