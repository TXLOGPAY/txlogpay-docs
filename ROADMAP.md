# TXLOGPAY Product Roadmap

## Overview

TXLOGPAY was designed as a programmable operational trade finance infrastructure platform.

The current MVP represents the initial operational validation layer of a broader infrastructure vision focused on:

* operational payment orchestration
* programmable settlement
* treasury automation
* operational finance infrastructure
* event-driven financial execution

This roadmap outlines the progressive evolution of the platform from:

* operational MVP
  toward:
* institutional-grade programmable trade finance infrastructure.

# MVP Limitations

The current MVP intentionally simulates several institutional integrations in order to validate:

* operational flows
* settlement logic
* orchestration architecture
* infrastructure feasibility

The following components are currently simulated:

* customs systems
* operational milestone APIs
* fiat on/off ramp providers
* institutional settlement orchestration
* treasury routing
* anchor infrastructure

This approach was intentionally chosen to prioritize:

* infrastructure validation
* workflow demonstration
* operational UX refinement
* programmable settlement logic

within hackathon delivery constraints.

# Infrastructure Evolution Strategy

TXLOGPAY was intentionally designed around progressive infrastructure replacement.

This allows:

* simulated modules
  to evolve into:
* institutional infrastructure

without requiring:

* frontend redesign
* operational workflow replacement
* UX restructuring

This modularity is a core architectural principle of the platform.
