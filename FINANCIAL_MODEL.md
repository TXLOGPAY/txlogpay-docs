# TXLOGPAY Financial Model

## Overview

TXLOGPAY was designed as a scalable operational finance infrastructure platform.

The business model combines:

* operational payment protection
* programmable settlement orchestration
* treasury automation
* operational monitoring
* enterprise infrastructure services

The platform monetizes international operational trade flows through infrastructure-based operational fees.

---

# Revenue Engine

TXLOGPAY revenue scales proportionally with:

* protected operational volume
* settlement orchestration complexity
* enterprise operational adoption
* infrastructure integrations

The core economic engine is based on:

```text id="fm1"
Protected Transaction Volume
×
Operational Fee Rate
```
